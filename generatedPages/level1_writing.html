Goals for this Level:

In this module, you will learn how to talk about what you like and don’t like with your friends and family. You will also be able to use sentences and words you already know in creative ways. Before you start the practice activities in any of the twelve topics below, begin with the strategies, grammar, and vocabulary to introduce you to the materials that will help you succeed in the practice activities.
