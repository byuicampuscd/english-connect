Speaking Level 3

Education

Goals: Focus Your Learning

Master the ability to:

  • Give a Simple Presentation
  • Tell a Story
  • Express Preferences
  • Express & Support Opinion
  • Explain Cause & Effect
  • Express Agreement and Disagreement
  • Make Simple Comparisons
  • Give Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Give a Simple Summary
  • Ask & Answer Questions – Who/What/When/Where/Why/How

Improve your ability to:

  • Give a Presentation
  • Narrate
  • Describe Objects, People, Places, and Events
  • Express & Support Opinion
  • Compare & Contrast Information
  • Give Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Summarize Information
  • Paraphrase Simple Ideas
  • Discuss Hypothetical Situations
  • Use Formal & Informal Speech
  • Respond Correctly to Unexpected Situations
