Speaking Level 2

Goals for this Level:

Master the ability to:

  • Express & Support Ideas
  • Tell a Story
  • Extend an Invitation
  • Coordinate Simple Plans
  • Make Basic Requests & Offers
  • Express & Support Opinions
  • Describe a Sequence of Events
  • Give a Simple Summary
  • Ask & Answer Questions – Who/What/When/Where/Yes/No

Improve your ability to:

  • Give a Simple Presentation
    • Introduction with Main Idea
    • Supporting Ideas
  • Tell a Story
  • Express Preferences
  • Express & Support Opinion
  • Explain Cause & Effect
  • Express Agreement and Disagreement
  • Make Simple Comparisons
  • Give Step-by-Step Instructions
  • Give a Simple Summary
  • Ask & Answer Questions – Who/What/When/Where/Why/How
