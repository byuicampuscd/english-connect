Writing Level 3

Ethics and Values

Goals: Focus Your Learning

Master the ability to write the following:

  • Create Simple Sentences
  • Create Simple Paragraphs
  • Express Preferences & Opinions
  • Describe Cause & Effect Relationships
  • Express Agreement & Disagreement
  • Make Comparisons
  • Describe a Sequence of Steps
  • Create a Summary & Reports
  • Ask & Repond to Questions Using Complex Sentence Structures

Improve your ability to:

  • Create a Simple Essay
    • Introductory Paragraph
    • Main Ideas & Supporting Ideas
  • Tell a Story
  • Describe People, Places, Objects, and Events
  • Express & Support Opinion
  • Compare & Contrast People, Places, Objects, and Events
  • Summarize & Report
  • Paraphrase
  • Use Informal & Formal Text
